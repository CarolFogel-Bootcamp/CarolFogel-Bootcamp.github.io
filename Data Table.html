Data Source

The data imported from the Data.Gov site contained birth year, name, gender and the number of occurences of the name for that birth year. Names having at least 5 occurences within the year were included in the dataset. All other names that had less than 5 occurences were omitted from the dataset.

The 10 data files used in the analysis of names on Social Security Card applications during the period 1960 - 1970 consist of a total of 138,955 records. Upon converting these records to an HTML table component, HTML was not able to display the page due to the excessive amount of table statements created.
