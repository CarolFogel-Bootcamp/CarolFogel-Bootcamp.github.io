Top 5 Male Names



The top 5 male names associated with Social Security Card Applications during the 1960 - 1970 time period are John, Michael, James, Robert and David. All 5 names' popularity increased and decreased during the 10 year period. All names, except Michael, showed a significant decrease of approximately 15,000 occurences of the name when compared from the year 1960 to the year 1970. Michael, on the other hand, showed a variance of approximately 8,000 occurences over the 10 year period and had a slightly higher occurence of the name in 1970 than in 1960, showing that the name's popularity was on the rise at the end of the 10 year period.

Although these names are the top 5 names on applications during this time period, it was surprising to discover that the percentages of these names with relation to all names on applications during this time period were very small. With a total of 7,931 unique names on applications during this time period, the name Michael made up only 4.32% of the applications. The names David, John, James, and Robert made up 3.81%, 3.7%, 3.55%, and 3.37%, respectively, of the applications.
