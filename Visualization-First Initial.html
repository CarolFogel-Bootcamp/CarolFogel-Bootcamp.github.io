Name Distribution by First Initial



Over 3 million occurences of male names associated with Social Security Card Applications during the 1960 - 1970 time period began with the letter 'J'. The letters 'R' and 'D', and 'M' (which correspond with the top 5 male names) followed with 2.3, 2.2 and 2 million occurences.

The first initial of female names with the greatest popularity was the letter 'L' with 1.96 million occurences followed by the letter 'S' with 1.94 million occurences. The letter 'M' with 1.85 million occurences and the letter 'C' with 1.78 million occurences followed. Even though Karen and Kimberly were among the top 5 female names, the letter 'K' had less occurrences (1.66 million) than the letter 'C', which was not in the top 5 female names. This would indicate that there were more female names beginning with the letter 'C' than female names beginning with the letter 'K' during this time period.
