Top 5 Female Names



The top 5 female names associated with Social Security Card Applications during the 1960 - 1970 time period are Lisa, Mary, Susan, Karen and Kimberly. During this time period, the name Kimberly grew in popularity, while the names Mary, Susan, and Karen declined in popularity. Only the name Lisa increased in popularity during this time period and then declined in popularity midway through this time period.

Although these names are the top 5 names on applications during this time period, it was surprising to discover that the percentages of these names with relation to all names on applications during this time period were very small. With a total of 14,237 unique female names on applications during this time period, the name Lisa made up only 2.72% of the applications. The names Mary, Susan, Karen, and Kimberly made up 1.94%, 1.57%, 1.56%, and 1.41%, respectively, of the applications.
